About Me

Hi, I’m Faith – a web developer and designer with a knack for turning complex code into simple, beautiful websites. I’m passionate about creating digital experiences that not only look good but work even better. From crafting seamless user interfaces to designing pixel-perfect layouts, I believe great design is all about making the web more enjoyable and accessible. With a background in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TailwindCSS, and UI/UX design, I’m constantly pushing myself to learn, grow, and innovate. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me diving into new design trends, sipping coffee, or wondering if my next idea will come from a late-night coding session (it probably will). I’m currently working toward my Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, and I’m excited to keep building projects that make an impact.
